We are the fulfilment of a prophecy made by well-known British missionary to Nigeria, Pa. Sydney Granville Elton in the 1930s about the Nigerian government paying her youths to preach the Gospel. "A time will come when the Nigerian youths will be paid to preach the gospel in the nooks and crannies of Nigeria"

Our Mission.
RURAL RUGGED EVANGELISM

Rural Rugged Evangelism is the responsibility and mission of every Jesus Corper in NCCF. It is a means of reaching souls for God's glory in the unreached rural areas, in the interiors of Nigeria, by the privilege of serving as Corps members.
